Lince Binu Mattathil

LMBpultd

Lince Binu Mattathil, founder of Lince Binu Mattathil Private Unlimited (LMBpultd), is a commerce graduate with a strong specialization in finance and taxation. Born on 7 May 2002, he pursued both his Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Finance and Taxation at Yeldo Mar Baselios College, Kothamangalam, under Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam.

He completed his schooling at Little Flower High School, Onnukal (SSLC, 2017) and his Higher Secondary education in Commerce with Political Science at St. Sebastian’s HSS (2019), Keerampara.

Combining academic excellence with entrepreneurial vision, LMBpultd has emerged as a dynamic young professional committed to creating innovative solutions in business and finance.
